ABOUT US

Discover Our Commitment to Compassionate Care and Comprehensive Solutions

At PinkCity Psychiatry, we are committed to providing compassionate and comprehensive mental healthcare services. Led by Dr. Kashish Thaper, MD Psychiatry, our team brings together expertise and dedication to support individuals on their journey to mental wellness.

With a foundation built on evidence-based medicine and a holistic approach, we strive to understand each patient's unique needs and offer personalized treatment plans. Our mission is to empower individuals to achieve optimal mental health and lead fulfilling lives.

Through a collaborative and empathetic approach, we address a wide range of psychiatric concerns, including depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, and more. We believe in fostering a supportive and non-judgmental environment where patients feel heard, valued, and respected.
