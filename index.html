ABOUT US

Discover Our Commitment to Compassionate Care and Comprehensive Solutions

At PinkCity Psychiatry, we are committed to providing compassionate and comprehensive mental healthcare services. Led by Dr. Kashish Thaper, MD Psychiatry, our team brings together expertise and dedication to support individuals on their journey to mental wellness.

With a foundation built on evidence-based medicine and a holistic approach, we strive to understand each patient's unique needs and offer personalized treatment plans. Our mission is to empower individuals to achieve optimal mental health and lead fulfilling lives.

Through a collaborative and empathetic approach, we address a wide range of psychiatric concerns, including depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, and more. We believe in fostering a supportive and non-judgmental environment where patients feel heard, valued, and respected.

About us

Our Services

PinkCity Psychiatry offers specialized Consultation Liaison Psychiatry services, bridging the gap between mental and physical health. Our team, led by Dr. Kashish Thaper, collaborates closely with medical specialists to address the interplay between mind and body in managing health challenges.

Our Services Include

Comorbid Substance Use Management

Our team specializes in addressing the complex relationship between substance use disorders and medical conditions. We offer personalized treatment plans that integrate psychiatric and medical care to support individuals in overcoming addiction while managing their physical health challenges effectively.

Medical Treatment Compliance

We understand the importance of adhering to medical treatments for optimal health outcomes. Our specialists work closely with patients to identify and address barriers to treatment adherence, providing support and strategies to ensure compliance with prescribed medications and therapies.

Decision Making Support

Making decisions about medical treatments can be overwhelming, especially when facing complex health issues. Our team provides expert guidance and support to patients and their families, empowering them to make informed decisions about their healthcare options based on their individual needs and preferences.

Illness-related Stress Management

Many individuals with medical illnesses also experience psychiatric symptoms or disorders. Our specialists are trained to recognize and address these comorbidities, offering integrated treatment plans that consider both physical and mental health aspects to optimize overall well-being.

Medication Management

Collaborating with specialists to minimize polypharmacy and optimize treatment outcomes.

Comorbid/Multimorbid Psychiatric Concerns

Diagnosing and managing psychiatric conditions alongside medical illnesses.
