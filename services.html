Consultation Liaison Services

PinkCity Psychiatry specializes in Consultation Liaison Psychiatry, bridging the gap between mental and physical health. Our team collaborates closely with medical specialists to address the interplay between mind and body in managing health challenges. Our Consultation Liaison services include:

1

Comorbid Substance Use Management

Addressing the complex relationship between substance use disorders and medical conditions to support recovery and overall well-being.

2

Medical Treatment Compliance

Providing support and strategies to ensure adherence to medical treatments for optimal health outcomes.

3

Decision Making Support

Offering expert guidance and support to empower patients in making informed decisions about their healthcare options.

4

Comorbid/Multimorbid Psychiatric Concerns

Addressing psychiatric concerns alongside medical illnesses to promote holistic health and well-being.

5

Illness-related Stress Management

Providing personalized support and coping strategies to help individuals manage stress related to their medical conditions.

6

Medication Management

Collaborating with medical specialists to optimize medication regimens and minimize potential drug interactions for improved treatment outcomes.
